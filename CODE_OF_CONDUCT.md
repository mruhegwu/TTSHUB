# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards
1. Treat everyone with respect.
2. Use welcoming and inclusive language.
3. Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
4. Give constructive feedback.
5. Accept constructive feedback.

## Our Responsibilities
1. Ensure that our online spaces are welcoming and inclusive.
2. Actively seek to promote an environment where everyone can participate equally.
3. Maintain an environment where individuals can be open and honest about their experiences.

## Scope
This code of conduct applies to everyone involved in our community, in all areas related to the project, and in all interactions, whether they be in person or online.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
